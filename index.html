Our Mission: Reforest the Planet, One Token at a Time.

We're leveraging blockchain technology to create a transparent, rewarding, and impactful system for global reforestation. Every tree planted is a verified, tangible step towards a greener future, and every contributor is a vital part of the ecosystem.

How It Works

1. Plant & Document

Individuals and organizations plant trees and capture photo or video evidence of their work.

2. Verify on the Chain

Evidence and GPS data are uploaded to our platform for verification, creating an on-chain proof-of-work.

3. Get Rewarded →

Click here to upload your proof and claim your ETH reward for contributing to a greener planet.
