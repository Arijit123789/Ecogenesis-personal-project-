The Art of
Regeneration

Planting a tree is precision engineering. Follow these protocols to ensure maximum survival rate and verify your proof-of-work.

01

Site Reconnaissance

Do not plant blindly. Analyze the terrain. Ensure the soil drains well and the location receives adequate sunlight. Avoid proximity to concrete foundations or overhead power lines.

02

Excavation

Precision matters. Dig a hole 2-3 times wider than the root ball, but no deeper. The root flare (where trunk meets roots) must remain visible above the soil line to prevent rot.

03

Execution & Verification

Place the sapling. Backfill with native soil. Water immediately to remove air pockets.

CRITICAL: Take a clear photo of your work. This is your "Proof of Planting" to mint your reward.
