Planting Protocol

Follow these steps to ensure survival and verification.

01

Select the Site

Choose a location with good drainage and sunlight. Ensure enough space for the tree to reach full maturity.

02

Dig Correctly

Dig a hole 2-3 times wider than the root ball, but no deeper. The root flare should sit slightly above ground level.

03

Plant & Verify

Backfill with native soil and water immediately. Take a photo of the planted tree to upload for your reward.
