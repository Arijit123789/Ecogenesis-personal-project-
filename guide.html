Field Manual

The Art of Planting.

Planting a tree is an act of hope. Do it correctly, and your impact will last for centuries.

1

Choose the Right Spot

Trees need space. Look up for power lines and down for pipes. Ensure the soil drains well and the spot gets adequate sunlight for the species you chose.

2

Dig the Hole

Dig a hole 2 to 3 times wider than the root ball, but no deeper. The "flare" (where roots meet trunk) should be slightly visible above ground level.

3

Plant & Verify

Place the tree gently. Backfill with native soil. Water immediately.
